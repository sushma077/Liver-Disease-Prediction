Some tips to prevent Liver Disease

Eat healthy foods

Eat a variety of healthy foods, including fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. Limit foods high in saturated fat, transfat, added sugars, salt and processed foods.Limit red meat and foods high in sugar or fructose.

Get Vaccinated

Get vaccinated against hepatitis A and B, as these viral diseases can lead to chronic conditions that can destroy your liver.

Be physically active

Regular physical activity can help you lose weight and improve your overall health. Try to get atleast 7 hours of sleep each night.

Avoid Alcohol

Limit alcohol consumption, as even a small amount can damage your liver.Avoid using illicit drugs, including marijuana, cocaine, heroin, hallucinogens, inhalants, and prescription-type psychotherapeutics used non-medically.

Be Careful with Medications

Avoid liver-toxic medications, such as over-the-counter pain killers, and only take medications that you need. Review all other medications with your doctor.
